What's New

  • Improved job board permission controls

    Control where Hide n' Seek is enabled: Instead of being enabled for all job boards upon install, you can now choose the job boards where you want Hide n' Seek enabled.

  • See what's new

    You'll now see what's new with each update, including new features and bug fixes, in a Hide n' Seek status page. You can open this page at any time to see what version you're running, which job boards have Hide n' Seek enabled, and even backup and restore your data.

  • Improved listing detection on LinkedIn

    You can now block listings on LinkedIn's job feed page (https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/)
