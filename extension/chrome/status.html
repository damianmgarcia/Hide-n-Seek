What's New

  • Update LinkedIn listing detection

    LinkedIn continues to change the structure of its site, which impacts Hide n' Seek's ability to detect listings. Hide n' Seek's detection will be updated as necessary to keep up.

    The size of the block buttons have been reduced so that they're less likely to obscure the listings that they're part of. Additionally, a tooltip was added to the block buttons to clarify that their function is to block listings by a specific company.
